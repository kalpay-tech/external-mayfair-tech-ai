# Our Process Images

This folder contains images for the "Our Process" section on the homepage.

## Required Images

Place the following images in this folder:

1. **step1.png** - Discovery & Analysis
   - Recommended: Office meeting, team discussion, or business analysis scene
   - Aspect ratio: 4:3 (e.g., 1200x900px)

2. **step2.png** - Strategy & Planning
   - Recommended: Planning session, whiteboard, or strategy meeting
   - Aspect ratio: 4:3 (e.g., 1200x900px)

3. **step3.png** - Development & Integration
   - Recommended: Coding, development team, or technology workspace
   - Aspect ratio: 4:3 (e.g., 1200x900px)

4. **step4.png** - Testing & Quality Assurance
   - Recommended: Testing environment, quality check, or monitoring dashboards
   - Aspect ratio: 4:3 (e.g., 1200x900px)

5. **step5.png** - Deployment & Training
   - Recommended: Team training, product launch, or deployment scene
   - Aspect ratio: 4:3 (e.g., 1200x900px)

6. **step6.png** - Optimization & Support
   - Recommended: Support team, analytics, or monitoring systems
   - Aspect ratio: 4:3 (e.g., 1200x900px)

## Image Guidelines

- **Format**: PNG or JPG (PNG recommended for better quality)
- **Dimensions**: Minimum 1200x900px (4:3 aspect ratio)
- **File Size**: Optimize for web (under 500KB per image)
- **Style**: Professional, modern, consistent with MayfairTech brand
- **Colors**: Images should complement the teal/green/navy color scheme

## How It Works

The images are automatically loaded from this folder by the Our Process component. Simply replace the placeholder images with your actual images, keeping the same filenames (step1.png, step2.png, etc.).

The layout alternates:
- Step 1, 3, 5: Text → Image
- Step 2, 4, 6: Image → Text

Images will update automatically when replaced - no code changes needed!
