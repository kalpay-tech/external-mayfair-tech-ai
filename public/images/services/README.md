# Our Services Images

This folder contains images for the "Our Services" section cards on the homepage.

## Required Images

Place the following images in this folder:

1. **ai-ml.jpg** - AI & Machine Learning
   - Suggested content: Neural networks, AI technology, machine learning concepts
   - Aspect ratio: 16:9 or 4:3
   - Dimensions: Minimum 800x600px

2. **e-business.jpg** - E-Business Solutions
   - Suggested content: E-commerce, online shopping, digital business
   - Aspect ratio: 16:9 or 4:3
   - Dimensions: Minimum 800x600px

3. **bpo.jpg** - BPO & Call Center
   - Suggested content: Customer service, call center operations, support team
   - Aspect ratio: 16:9 or 4:3
   - Dimensions: Minimum 800x600px

4. **logistics.jpg** - Smart Logistics
   - Suggested content: Warehouse, shipping, supply chain, logistics operations
   - Aspect ratio: 16:9 or 4:3
   - Dimensions: Minimum 800x600px

## Image Guidelines

- **Format**: JPG or PNG (JPG recommended for photos)
- **Dimensions**: Minimum 800x600px
- **File Size**: Optimize for web (under 500KB per image)
- **Style**: Professional, relevant to each service
- **Colors**: Should work well with white text overlay

## How It Works

The images will be displayed at the top of each service card with:
- Title overlaid on the image in white text
- Gradient overlay for text readability
- Hover zoom effect
- Automatic fallback gradient if images are missing

Simply add your images with the exact filenames above!
